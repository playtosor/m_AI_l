# common_conventions — Local installation conventions

> This file is read automatically at the start of each session (via `commun.md`).
> It is specific to your installation and is **not distributed on GitHub**.
> Fill it in as your setup takes shape — it can stay empty until you need it.

---

## What belongs here

Anything specific to your local environment that threads should know:

- **Paths** — where your projects live, your common folder, any shared resources
- **Navigation rules** — folders that are off-limits, read-only zones, generated folders to ignore
- **Session conventions** — habits specific to your team (e.g. `M!` for mobile mode)
- **Environment notes** — OS, tools in use, constraints to keep in mind
- **Project-specific shortcuts** — handles, aliases, recurring references
